# Section prompt

## Task

Produce a **Market Landscape** section with a clear market definition, alternatives, market norms, and adoption drivers and barriers.

## Workflow

1. Define the market:
   - Take Primary domain
   - Provide a recognized market/category name that fits the Problem Statement and User Segments.

2. List alternatives:
   - Direct competitor segments
   - Indirect competitor segments
   - Substitutes segments
3. Describe market norms:
   - Baseline capabilities users assume
   - Standard UX/service expectations
4. Identify adoption drivers and barriers:
   - Drivers: what pushes usage or demand
   - Barriers: what slows or blocks adoption
5. Validate and filter:
   - Remove anything not supported by Inputs or domain-generic realities.
   - Exclude solutions, features, or differentiation language.

## Generation Rules

- Use clear, simple, non-technical language.
- Do NOT use in the segment name specific company or product names.
- Do NOT invent statistics, growth rates, named regulations, or technologies.
- All items must be general, defensible, and traceable.
