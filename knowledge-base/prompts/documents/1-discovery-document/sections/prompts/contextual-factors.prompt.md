# Section prompt

## Task

Produce a **Contextual Factors** section that captures the most relevant environmental factors and circumstances that influences the problem space.

## Contextual factors

Group factors by relevance. You may use concise groups such as:

- Global (trends, societal objectives, global problems, broad processes)
- Market (trends, conditions, competition, norms, pressures)
- Operational (process dependencies, capability gaps)
- Ecosystem (partners, platforms, dependencies)
- Temporal (historical,timing drivers, recent shifts)

## Workflow

1. Identify the most influential contextual factors from Inputs and domain-generic realities.
2. For each group, list factor names and descriptions.

## Generation Rules

- Use clear, simple, non-technical language.
- Do NOT introduce solutions, features, UX ideas, or architectures.
- Do NOT invent named regulations, technologies, or metrics.
- Keep groups concise and focused on influence.
